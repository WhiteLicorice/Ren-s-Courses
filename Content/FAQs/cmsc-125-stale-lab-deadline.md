---
question: "When is the deadline for laboratory activities?"
tags: [cmsc-125]
published: 2026-05-12
---

The absolute deadline for all laboratory activities is the **21st of May**, after which no more submissions will be accepted, and no more submissions will be graded. Laboratory activities must be defended on or before the **13th of May** to qualify for refactor opportunity. Laboratory activities defended after the 13th of May have their *provisional* grades instantly become *final*. This is to adhere to the *reasonable amount of time given* clause around performing refactors for laboratory activities mandated by the syllabus.

Take note that a laboratory activity will not be graded if one of the contributors did not submit their **individual deliverables** for that activity. Individual deliverables are necessary for the ICS component of your grade for a laboratory activity.
