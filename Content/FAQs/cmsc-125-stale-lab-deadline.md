---
question: "When is the deadline for laboratory activities?"
tags: [cmsc-125]
published: 2026-05-12
---

Laboratory activities must be defended on or before the **13th of May** to qualify for refactor opportunity. Laboratory activities defended after the 13th of May have their *provisional* grades instantly become *final*. This is to adhere to the *reasonable amount of time given* clause around performing refactors for laboratory activities mandated by the syllabus. The absolute deadline for all laboratory activities is the **21st of May**, after which no more submissions will be accepted, and no more submissions will be graded.

Take note that the a laboratory activity will not be graded if one of the contributors has not submitted their individual deliverables for that activity. This is necessary for the ICS component of your grade for a laboratory activity.
