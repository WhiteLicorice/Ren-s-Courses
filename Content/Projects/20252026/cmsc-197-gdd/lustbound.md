abstract: "Lustbound is a 2D top-down survival game built in Godot 4, set in a cyberpunk city. The player controls a succubus who must use a charm aura to feed on NPCs in order to survive a starvation timer that drains health when feeding stops. NPCs operate on a multi-state AI with idle, wander, threat-detection, chase, attack, and husk states. The game features a leveling system with XP progression, multiple stage maps connected by access zones, and escalating danger as the player's level increases. The succubus's charm ability draws NPCs within an aura radius, enabling feeding through dedicated feed zones."
