abstract: "Flappy Joyride is a Flappy Bird clone built in Godot 4 that introduces ballistic hazards and dynamically oriented pipe obstacles. Pipes spawn with randomized gap positions and one of four orientations — Normal, Horizontal, Diagonal Left, and Diagonal Right — significantly altering the navigation geometry of each run. The game's primary mechanical addition is a Missile System: homing projectiles that spawn at a Y-position relative to the player's current location, travel faster than the environment scroll speed, and are preceded by a telegraphed warning indicator with animated sprite and audio. Difficulty scales automatically over time through increasing scroll speed and accelerating hazard spawn rates."
