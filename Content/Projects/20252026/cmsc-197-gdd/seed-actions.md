abstract: "Seed Actions is a turn-based strategy RPG prototype built in Godot 4, centered on party building, resource-driven combat, elemental typing, and simultaneous action planning. The player navigates a world map, enters battle gates, assembles a party of up to five heroes from an unlockable roster, and clears multi-wave battle stages. The defining structural choice is simultaneous resolution: all heroes and enemies commit actions before a unified combat resolution phase executes, rewarding planning over reaction. Gameplay content — heroes, enemies, abilities, effects, value formulas, stages, and world locations — is entirely data-driven through Godot Resource files. Systems include a skill, guard, and ultimate cooldown economy; buff, debuff, and condition status processing; world map navigation with warp gates and service nodes; BGM and SFX autoload managers; and a hero-unlock progression system tied to cleared battle nodes."
